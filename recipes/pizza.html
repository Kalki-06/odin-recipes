Pizza

Description

Pizza, an enduring favorite across continents, is a celebration of simplicity and flavor. A crisp, thin crust or fluffy dough forms the canvas for a medley of ingredients: tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and a selection of toppings ranging from savory pepperoni to fresh vegetables and fragrant herbs. Baked to perfection in a blazing hot oven, each slice offers a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Whether enjoyed as a quick bite or a shared meal with friends, pizza's universal appeal lies in its ability to satisfy cravings with its comforting, cheesy goodness and endless customization options.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
  6. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
